## Sapling Biome Lock
- Saplings, Cactus and Bamboo will only grow (when placed by a player) in the biomes in which they naturally grow
- If the biome is incorrect and player uses bonemeal, smoke particles will appear and player will get an advancement telling him to grow it in correct biome
## Automatable Resources Biome Lock
- Asurine, Bauxite, Crimsite, Fireclay, Galena, Ochrum and Veridium will generate naturally in respective biomes (except Lignite, it will be everywhere in the overworld).
