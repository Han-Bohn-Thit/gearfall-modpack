## Sapling Biome Lock
- Saplings, Cactus and Bamboo will only grow (when placed by a player) in the biomes in which they naturally grow
- If the biome is incorrect and the player uses bonemeal, smoke particles will appear, and the player will get an advancement telling him to grow it in the correct biome
## Automatable Resources Biome Lock
- Asurine, Bauxite, Crimsite+Fireclay, Ochrum and Veridium will generate naturally in respective biomes (except Lignite, which will be everywhere in the overworld).
